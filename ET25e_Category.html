Subsea Thruster Motors

1200W High-Thrust Propulsion for Research and Industrial ROVs

Eustance Technology introduces the ET-25e brushless underwater thruster engineered for professional and research-grade underwater vehicles requiring maximum performance. This advanced propulsion system features an exterior-rotor brushless DC motor with 7 pole pairs, delivering up to 19.2 kgf forward thrust and 16.0 kgf reverse thrust at 24V DC with exceptional torque density and energy efficiency. Built with precision-machined 316L stainless steel components and carbon fiber-reinforced nylon blades, the ET-25e incorporates a double-compensated rotary dynamic seal and oil-pressure compensation system for maintenance-free operation up to 300-meter depth, ensuring quiet, low-vibration performance with superior corrosion resistance in demanding marine environments.

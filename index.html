Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Find answers to common questions about our marine robotics products

We offer a comprehensive range of underwater thrusters designed for various applications. Our product line includes the ET03e (2.7kgf thrust), ET05 (5kgf thrust), ET07e, and heavy-duty 6.5kgf models. All thrusters feature corrosion-resistant SS316 construction, composite bearings, and are rated for depths up to 250 meters. Each model is optimized for different payload requirements and operational conditions.

Absolutely! We specialize in customization and work closely with clients to modify thrusters, ESC controllers, and complete ROV systems to meet specific mission requirements. Our engineering team can adjust thrust specifications, voltage ranges, mounting configurations, and integrate specialized sensors or components. Contact us with your project details for a customized solution.

Brushless thrusters, which we exclusively manufacture, offer significant advantages over brushed motors. They provide higher efficiency (up to 30% more), longer lifespan due to no mechanical wear on brushes, better power-to-weight ratios, and smoother operation with less vibration. Brushless motors also generate less electrical noise, making them ideal for underwater applications where precision control is critical.

Our standard thruster models are rated for depths up to 150-250 meters depending on the model. The ET03e and ET05 are rated for 150m, while our heavy-duty ET07e and 6.5kgf models can operate at 200-250m depths. For deeper applications beyond these ratings, we offer custom solutions with enhanced pressure-resistant sealing and reinforced construction.

Our thrusters work with our proprietary waterproof ESC controllers (45A, 2-6S LiPo compatible) featuring bi-directional control and support for PWM, Oneshot, Multishot, and Dshot protocols. They're also compatible with standard brushless ESCs from major manufacturers. Our ESCs feature auto-detect throttle signals, regenerative braking, and hardware-generated motor PWM for reduced noise and improved precision control.

Thruster selection depends on your ROV's size, weight, operational depth, and required maneuverability. For basic 4-DOF control (forward/backward, left/right, up/down, yaw), you'll need 4-6 thrusters. Full 6-DOF control requires 6-8 thrusters. Consider payload weight, desired speed, current conditions, and mission duration. Our technical team can help you calculate thrust requirements and recommend optimal configurations.

Regular maintenance includes rinsing with freshwater after saltwater use, inspecting propellers for debris or damage, checking cable connections, and testing seals periodically. Our SS316 construction and composite bearings minimize corrosion and wear. We recommend annual seal inspection for frequently used thrusters and immediate inspection after any impact or abnormal operation. Detailed maintenance guides are provided with each product.

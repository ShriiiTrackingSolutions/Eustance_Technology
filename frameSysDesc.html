Description

Frame systems provide the essential backbone for ROV construction, engineered from marine-grade materials like anodized aluminum, titanium, or HDPE that resist corrosion in harsh saltwater environments. These lightweight yet robust structures feature pre-drilled modular mounting points for easy integration of thrusters, cameras, sensors, and electronics. Available in open-frame and box-frame configurations, each design offers hydrodynamic profiles that minimize drag and maximize maneuverability underwater. The frame maintains optimal weight distribution by supporting heavy components at the bottom and buoyancy materials at the top, ensuring stability during operations. Compatible with standardized mounting interfaces, these frames accommodate various payload configurations and allow quick customization for different mission requirements. Perfect for custom builds, upgrades, or replacement applications in depths ranging from 100m to 600m.

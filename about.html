About Eustance Technology

Eustance Technology is a pioneering manufacturer specializing in the research, development, and production of advanced underwater propulsion systems and marine robotics components. Based in Vadodara, Gujarat, India, our core business encompasses the design and manufacturing of high-performance subsea thrusters, electronic speed controllers (ESC), complete ROV systems, and precision-engineered marine accessories.

Our mission is to empower ocean exploration and underwater operations through innovative, reliable, and cost-effective propulsion technologies that meet the evolving demands of marine research, commercial diving, aquaculture, defense, and industrial applications.
